About Us

Who are we?

In early 2022, we started the Gnarly Skate School. We are a friendly group of very expierienced skateboarders. Our goal is to get more kids involved in skateboarding, and to show them how fun it can be. And hopefully, they will love skateboarding as much as we do.
We are all old proffessional skateboarders and have more than 20 years of experience. We only do private lessons that are booked by signing up on this website.

Why skate with us?

We offer a safe, educational and fun environment for your kids to learn skateboarding. Our goal is to bring more and more kids to the skateboarding scene. We love what we do and we love kids, and it shows in our lessons. We hope you want to be a part of this community. And we promise you this. You will see results.
